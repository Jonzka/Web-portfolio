About me

I easily get along with all different kinds of people from different backgrounds, and I work well in teams. I did martial arts for seven years in my teenage years, toward the end of which I assisted in teaching juniors. I learn new things fairly quickly, and in my work prefer to get things done rather than laze around.

Education and work

I graduated from junior high in 2019, and went on to study in upper secondary right after. I completed my matriculation exams in upper secondary in 2022 with an average of M. I then took half a year to work at a storage and delayed going to university of applied sciences, because my service in the Finnish defense forces started in January 2023.

After completing my service in June 2023 I had a summer break and started my studies in JAMK.

During summer breaks in upper secondary, I worked at a small local renovation company. It was very physical work, and mostly involved cleaning places and carrying heavy objects.
